The why:

Years ago, when I had no responsibilities and plenty of time, I did NaNoWriMo and somehow manage to finish it, but I wouldn't say the end product was much of anything. The fun part was setting a goal that was very difficult with for no other reason that to see if I could do it.

A million years passed, and I have plenty of responsibilities but a little time and remembered that NaNoWriMo existed just in time to take part this years. SO, while I'm out of practice and have no real idea what I'm going to do, I decided to embrace the fun of setting seemingly pointless goals and have given myself the weekend before NaNoWriMo to trying to built this little django app to write the book on. Because while I'm not very good at writing, I'm also not very good at webdev either so why not combine the two and make my life more difficult.
